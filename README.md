# Phone Stand

A compact and portable 3D-printable phone stand designed for everyday use. Its minimalist design provides a stable platform for your smartphone while remaining lightweight, durable, and easy to print.

## Overview

This phone stand is designed to securely hold most smartphones in both **portrait** and **landscape** orientations, making it suitable for a variety of everyday tasks. Whether you're watching videos, attending video calls, reading, browsing the web, or following recipes, the stand offers a convenient hands-free viewing experience.

## Features

* Compact and lightweight design for easy portability
* Supports both portrait and landscape orientations
* Stable base to help keep your phone securely in place
* Simple, clean design that is easy to 3D print
* Compatible with most smartphones

## Printing

The model is optimized for FDM 3D printing and can be printed using common materials such as PLA or PETG. It prints as a single part and can be produced in one color or multiple colors to match your preference.
